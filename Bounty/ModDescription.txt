Bounty Mode

Eliminate players to earn bounties as bonus armies.

The game creator can choose one of four bounty styles:
- Fixed bounty: every credited elimination pays the same amount.
- Global bounty growth: every credited elimination increases everyone's bounty.
- Kill-count bounty growth: each player's bounty grows from their own number of credited kills.
- Stacking bounty: when a player eliminates someone, that player's bounty is added to the killer's own bounty.

Kills are attributed to the decisive order:
- Successful attack: attacker gets the reward
- Failed attack where the attacker is eliminated: defender gets the reward
- Blockade/Abandon trap kills into neutral: trap owner gets the reward

Trap attribution on a territory is removed as soon as that territory is conquered by any player.
