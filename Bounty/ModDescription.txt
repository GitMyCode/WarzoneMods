Bounty Mode

Eliminate players to earn rewards.

Each time you eliminate another player, you get a configurable fixed reward:
- Normal games: fixed troops reward
- Commerce games: fixed gold reward

Kills are attributed to the decisive order:
- Successful attack: attacker gets the reward
- Failed attack where the attacker is eliminated: defender gets the reward
- Blockade/Abandon trap kills into neutral: trap owner gets the reward

Trap attribution on a territory is removed as soon as that territory is conquered by any player.
