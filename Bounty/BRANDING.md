# Bounty Mod — Branding

## Mod Description

**Bounty Mode** — Get rewarded for eliminating players.

Every elimination earns the killer a bonus: troops in normal games, gold in
commerce games. Aggressive play is rewarded — turtling behind borders won't
win you the resource advantage. Hunt down your opponents.

---

## Logo Prompt

Use with ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney, or similar image generators.

### Primary prompt

```
Flat icon, game-icons.net style: white silhouette on a dark background.

Subject: a "WANTED" bounty poster with a skull on it, slightly tilted or
pinned to a surface with a knife or dagger through it. The poster has torn
edges. Below the skull, a simple banner or tag showing a coin/reward symbol.

Style constraints:
- Single-color silhouette (white on dark charcoal/navy #1a1a2e or #0d1b2a)
- No gradients, no outlines — filled shapes only
- Thick, bold shapes readable at 64x64 pixels
- Geometric and clean, not hand-drawn
- No text on the poster (just the skull + coin symbol)
- Square format with rounded corners or circular crop

Reference: game-icons.net icon style — high contrast, chunky silhouettes,
designed for game UI at small sizes.

Color option: if adding accent color, use a single warm gold (#d4a847) for
the coin/reward element only, everything else stays white on dark.
```

### Alternate concepts (pick one if the above doesn't land)

**Concept B — Crosshair + coin stack**
```
Flat icon, game-icons.net style. White silhouette on dark background.
A crosshair/target reticle with a small stack of coins at the center
instead of the usual dot. Clean, geometric, readable at 64x64.
```

**Concept C — Skull with a price tag**
```
Flat icon, game-icons.net style. White silhouette on dark background.
A skull in profile or front-facing, with a hanging price tag or coin
attached to it by a string. Minimalist, no detail — just the shape.
```

**Concept D — Dagger through a coin**
```
Flat icon, game-icons.net style. White silhouette on dark background.
A dagger stabbed through a large coin. Simple, aggressive, reads the
"kill = reward" concept instantly.
```

### Size and format

- Export as PNG, 256x256 minimum (Warzone will scale down)
- Current Assassin icon is ~64x64, cyan on dark — Bounty should be
  similar size but distinct in shape
- If possible, generate at 512x512 and downscale for crispness

### What to avoid

- Realistic/photographic style — needs to be flat icon
- Too many small details — must read at 64x64
- Red blood/gore — keep it game-friendly
- Text in the icon — won't be legible at small sizes
- Copying the Assassin icon (crosshair + person) — needs its own identity
