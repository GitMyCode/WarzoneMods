Limited Private Diplomacy turns private communication into a scarce, visible strategic action.

Players use a Communication Card from the normal Warzone Cards UI. Playing the card opens a dialog where the sender chooses one player and writes a private message. The card resolves late in the turn, then the recipient can read the message in their mod inbox and the sender keeps a sent copy.

Other players cannot read the content, but the public diplomacy log and turn event show that Player A sent a private message to Player B.

This creates a fog of diplomacy: everyone knows communication happened, but nobody else knows whether it was honest negotiation, deception, a signal, or bait.

This mod automatically disables built-in private messaging when the game is created, so private diplomacy happens through Communication Cards instead.
